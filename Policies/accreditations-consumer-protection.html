Accreditations and Consumer Protections

Vibra Energy Ltd. proudly holds TrustMark accreditation. TrustMark, a not-for-profit social enterprise operating under a Master Licence Agreement from the Government's Department for Business & Trade, ensures that registered businesses adhere to specified practices. For homeowners, engaging a TrustMark Registered Business offers assurance and confidence, while for trades, it signifies quality and professionalism.

Vibra Energy Ltd. is PAS 2030 certified, adhering to the standard's requirements for installing energy efficiency measures in domestic retrofit projects. This specification and guidance are essential for firms undertaking Green Deal and ECO installations, government schemes aimed at reducing household energy bills and carbon emissions.

As an esteemed member of the Renewable Energy Consumer Code (RECC), Vibra Energy Ltd. ensures that consumers considering small-scale heat or power generation units for their homes can make informed choices with confidence. RECC sets service standards to provide consumers with necessary confidence in renewable energy installations.

Vibra Energy Ltd.'s work is covered by Bluedrop Services (NW) Ltd, an authorised and regulated insurance broker by the Financial Conduct Authority. This insurance broker specialises in providing both deposit protection and insurance backed guarantees for home improvement installations, ensuring that our workmanship guarantee is underwritten and providing cover for a defined period.

What is an Insurance Backed Guarantee?

An insurance backed guarantee offers valuable protection in the event Vibra Energy Ltd. cannot honour its written guarantee. It underwrites our workmanship guarantee and provides cover for the defined period.

Prospective Accredited Installers must demonstrate competency, financial solvency, and relevant certification or experience for installed products. As an Accredited Installer, Vibra Energy Ltd. undergoes regular monitoring to ensure financial solvency and compliance with Protection Terms of Business Agreement and Quality Assurance Frameworks.

Why Choose an Installer Offering Insurance Backed Guarantees?

Selecting an installer offering insurance backed guarantees provides additional peace of mind, ensuring protection in unexpected circumstances. By choosing Vibra Energy Ltd., you entrust your interests to a responsible installer committed to your confidence and satisfaction.
