Electric Vehicle Charging Points

If you want your electric vehicle charged and ready to go each morning, then installing a charging point at your home is the solution for you. Charging at home is the simplest and most popular way to charge an electric vehicle. In addition, Government grants are now available making it even more affordable to have your own charge point at home.

Because the charging point is connected to a live electricity feed it is essential that you choose a company with accredited, qualified electricians. Our electricians have the necessary experience and knowledge to carry out a safe and efficient installation.


How Long Does EV Charger Installation Take?

An electric car charger installation typically takes just a few hours. Often the EV charger installation company will have assessed and established a suitable location for the chargepoint, and then decide with you on an appropriate cable run.


How Long Does A Charge Take?

A slow charge rate of a completely depleted battery (under 3 kW) using a 3pin plug will take 6-8 hours to fully charge. This can be longer for models with larger batteries.

A more efficient and preferable method is to have a fast charge charge point installed. These offer a charge time of 3-4hours for a fully depleted battery using a 7 kW charge point.


Are you Eligible For An OLEV Grant?

Are you Eligible For An OLEV Grant? If you are eligible for the OLEV grant Vibra Energy can reduce the cost of your charger installation by up to £500. To find out if you are eligible you will need to initially answer ‘yes’ to the 4 questions:

  1. Have you ordered an electric car that is due to arrive in the next 4 months? Or do you already have possession of an electric car?
  2. Did you purchase your electric vehicle after the 1st October 2016?
  3. Do you intend to keep the vehicle for 6 months or longer from the date you of ownership?
  4. Do you have private off-street parking where we can install your chargepoint without running the cable across a public footpath?
